## About the Project

Rosavo is a Vue.js PWA fashion store template designed for fast, modern, and mobile-friendly shopping experiences. It works seamlessly across all modern browsers and devices, allowing you to launch your fashion eCommerce store quickly with all the essential pages and features included.
